Project Overview

Sivee is a Laravel 11 web application for job discovery and candidate profile management, with a separate admin panel for managing users, companies, and scraped job listings.

The platform has two main roles:

  • Candidate/User: signs up, completes onboarding, manages profile/CV/skills, browses jobs, and tracks job interactions.
  • Admin: logs into a separate admin guard and manages users, companies, and jobs through DataTables-powered CRUD screens.

Core app routes and role split are defined in:

  • routes/web.php
  • config/auth.php

Business Flows Implemented

Candidate-facing flows

The candidate experience covers account creation, verification, onboarding, profile completion, CV management, job discovery, and engagement tracking.

  • User authentication with signup, login, forgot/reset password, and logout.
  • Google OAuth login through Socialite.
  • Email verification using signed verification URLs.
  • Multi-step onboarding for personal details, profile photo, job preferences, and CV upload.
  • Profile management for summaries, external links, languages, skills, work experience, education, certificates, and cover letters.
  • Multiple CV management with default CV selection and auto-submission preferences.
  • Searchable and filterable job listings by query, location, and category.
  • Job detail pages, company pages, list/detail rendering modes, and popularity sorting.
  • Job views recorded in job_views and CTA clicks tracked in user_click_tracks.

Primary controllers:

  • app/Http/Controllers/AuthController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/ProfileController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/HomeController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/JobController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/NotificationController.php

Admin-facing flows

The admin area gives operators a separate control surface for platform management, content maintenance, scraping, and engagement analytics.

  • Admin login through a separate admin guard.
  • Dashboard analytics for monthly total clicks and per-company click stats segmented by button type.
  • User management with listing, activation/deactivation, and delete actions.
  • Company management with metadata/logo editing, active-state controls, delete actions, and cascading job status updates.
  • Job management with listing, activation/deactivation, delete actions, and manual scrape triggers for selected companies.

Primary admin controllers:

  • app/Http/Controllers/Admin/HomeController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/Admin/UserController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/Admin/CompanyController.php
  • app/Http/Controllers/Admin/JobController.php

Job Scraping Architecture

Sivee imports jobs through domain-specific scraper endpoints configured in scrape_domains.

The scheduled scraper iterates active scrape domains, finds matching company records, marks older active jobs inactive for that domain/company pair, and inserts the latest normalized listings.

  • ScrapeDomain::scrapeJobs() builds a request to https://{API_URL}/api/extract-jobs with url, site_id, and limit.
  • Job payloads are normalized and inserted or upserted by CompanyJob::InsertJobs().
  • The scheduled command app:scrape-jobs runs daily to keep listings fresh.

Relevant files:

  • app/Models/ScrapeDomain.php
  • app/Models/CompanyJob.php
  • app/Console/Commands/ScrapeJobs.php
  • routes/console.php

Data Model Summary

Main entities:

  • users, admins
  • companies, company_jobs, scrape_domains
  • job_infos, user_c_v_s, cover_letters
  • skills, languages, pivot tables (user_skills, user_languages)
  • work_experiences, user_education, user_certificates
  • job_views, user_click_tracks
  • Laravel defaults for auth/session/cache/queue/notifications

Notable relationships:

  • User has one jobInfo, many CVs, many profile sub-records, many-to-many skills/languages.
  • Company has many jobs.
  • CompanyJob belongs to company, has many views/clicks.

Migrations are in database/migrations and include custom schema additions from 2024-06 through 2025-01.

Security and Access Patterns

  • Separate auth guards for users and admins in config/auth.php.
  • Route-level middleware for onboarding and launch-gated screens, including CheckBoardingDone and ComingSoonMiddleware.
  • Email verification support through MustVerifyEmail on the user model.
  • Password hashing through model casts.

Notification and Email Flows

  • Account-created notifications through mail and database channels.
  • Verification email delivery with temporary signed URLs.
  • Reset-password email delivery through the password broker route.
  • Queued email jobs including SendAccountCreatedEmailJob, SendVerificationEmailJob, and SendResetPasswordEmailJob.
